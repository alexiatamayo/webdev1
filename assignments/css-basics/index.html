OVERVIEW

Squid Game is a popular South Korean Netflix Drama directed by Hwang Dong-hyuk that was released on September 17, 2021. The show revolves around a group of people who accept to compete in children’s games for a chance to win a cash prize, but the stakes are deadly. It can best be described as a violent test of morality and humanity. The show has been watched by millions and is currently ranked #1 on Netflix’s trending page. The show is on the verge of becoming Netflix’s most-watched series in history.

Jung Ho-Yeon

One of the show’s breaking actresses is Jung Ho-Yeon. Jung Ho-Yeon was born and raised in Seoul, South Korea, and is known as a popular model for luxury clothing brands and is currently being labeled as Korea’s next top model. Before the show, she only had 400,000 Instagram followers and in over 3 weeks she has accumulated 15 million followers. Jung Ho-Yeon made her acting debut in Squid Game playing Sae-Byeok, a young North Korean immigrant living in Seoul day by day with her little brother.

Wi Ha-Joon

Another break-out star from the show is Wi Ha-Joon. Wi Ha-Joon was born and raised in Soan-myeon Wando-gun, South Korea, and is currently signed to MSTeam Entertainment. He is an actor and model best known for his role in the television series, Something in the Rain, Romance Is A Bonus Book, 18 Again and now Squid Game which has launched him into international fame.

WHY YOU SHOULD WATCH

Squid Game will make you cry, laugh, and constantly leave you on the edge of your seat. The first season contains 9 episodes with each episode about an hour long. This show is highly recommended and definitely binge-worthy!
